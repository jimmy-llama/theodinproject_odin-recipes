Air Fryer Frittata

Home


Description

Super simple and delicious breakfast that cooks up in minutes with an air fryer!

Credit: Farah Abumaizar, Every Little Crumb

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 medium (about 7 ounces each) multicolored bell peppers, cored and cut into 1 to 1 1/2-inch pieces (about 2 cups)
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • more stuff

Steps

  1. Line the air fryer with parchment paper
  2. Crack eggs into it
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir
  4. Cook at 350F for 15 minutes or until done
